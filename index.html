About Scinnova

WHAT IS SCINNOVA?

From Notions to Infinity, Innovating Realms

Scinnova is the flagship event of Cedar College’s STEM Societies. It is a legacy passed down for seven years, nourished and improved by new minds every year. A science competition like no other, it’s where one crosses the limitations of theory and STEM in its purest form.

The word "Scinnova" stems from the words "Science" and "Innovation." The vision behind this is to challenge young minds in General Sciences, Programming and Robotics, Psychology, and much more.

Scinnova is more than just a Science Olympiad; it ignites the flames of passion for science in an individual. It lets you explore yourself and venture STEM beyond theory and connects you with the essence of these domains.

From a participant’s standpoint, Scinnova will be an experience to remember. Taking ideas out of the classroom and putting them in front of you. Create complex circuitry. Dissect creatures with paper-thin precision. Unravel conspiracies.

In Scinnova, rediscover yourself, go outside your comfort zone and get a firsthand experience of how it feels to work in the practical world.

Mathematics

Ptolemy's Puzzle

Ptolemy’s Puzzle awaits, where you'll harness your analytical and critical thinking skills to solve captivating math problems. This module will lead you to explore the mysteries of the universe through the power of numbers. Conquer the towering peaks of algebra, navigate intricate labyrinths of geometry, and soar with the eagles of calculus. Unleash your inner mathematician and delve into the mesmerizing world of logic, where numbers dance and equations sing

Physics

Planck's Paradox

Dive into the captivating world of physics with Planck’s Paradox. Here, you'll face perplexing problems that challenge your practical faculties and quick-thinking skills. If you can comprehend complex equations under immense pressure and have a drive to explore physical phenomena, then hold onto your stopwatches and ready your rulers, as Planck's Paradox is sure to make your head spin.

Arcanum

Crime and Cryptogaphy

Join us in Arcanum, where you'll find yourself amidst a mysterious crime scene with a cunning mastermind on the loose. This module will test your detective skills to their limits, as you decipher intricate codes, decode cryptic messages, and uncover hidden signs in plain sight. Are you ready to take on the challenge? Grab your magnifying glass and embrace your inner Sherlock Holmes!

Chemistry

Illuiminatio

Step into the world of Illuminatio, where you'll journey through the intriguing realm of chemistry. As you navigate intricate reaction pathways filled with challenges, you'll craft captivating colors and wield flames at your command. Each step you take becomes a catalyst, drawing you nearer to the answers you seek. Approach each question as an experiment, breaking it down to uncover the mysteries within. Be prepared to stretch your wit and intuition to their fullest extent.

Biology

Asclepius

In Asclepius, dive into the captivating world of biology, exploring the secrets of life, genetics, and the incredible saga of evolution that has shaped our planet. This module will test your biological knowledge and your ability to communicate scientific information uniquely. Get ready to demonstrate your creativity and expertise across the broad spectrum of biology, covering topics from genetics to ecology and beyond.

Astronomy

Redshift

Explore the wonders of the cosmos in Redshift. With countless celestial bodies dotting the sky, you'll embark on a mesmerizing journey to make sense of other dimensions, galaxies, extraterrestrial life, and the vast cosmos. Unravel the mysteries hidden in this expansive void and enlighten yourself and others, or risk being eclipsed.

Psychology

Freud's Forsit

Join us in Freud's Forsit to unravel the mysteries of human behavior and cognition. This module will challenge you to navigate through numerous minds and understand how they work. Dive deep into the enthralling realm of psychology, where every mind is waiting to be explored and understood. This module will push your analytical skills to their full potential.
